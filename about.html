ABOUT

The Automated Soil Health Assessment App is a mobile-based application designed to provide farmers and agricultural specialists with a fast, cost-effective, and accurate way to assess soil health. The app leverages machine learning (ML) and satellite data integration to analyze soil properties such as moisture, organic content, and nutrient levels. It aims to replace traditional, time-consuming, and expensive soil testing methods with a non-invasive, real-time solution.
